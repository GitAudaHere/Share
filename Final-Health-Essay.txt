Introduction

Sexual education is taught in many health classes in order to help students make the right decisions when it comes to their sexual behaviors; however, many students disregard this topic in school and underapreciate its value in their lives. A few of the most important topics in sexual education are healthy relationships, coercion and consent, and pregnancy. These three topics are very beneficial for high school juniors, who are approaching the legal age of consent and becoming responsible adults of society. Teaching high school students about healthy relationships, coercion and consent, and pregnancy will effectively prepare them for the expections of society.
